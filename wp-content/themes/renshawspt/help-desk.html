FAQ Check here for answers to frequently asked questions

  • How Do I Change/Update My Billing Information?

    Use the appropriate tabs above to update or change your account profile information.

  • How Do I Change My Password?

    You can change your password by clicking the “UPDATE” button next to Account Details at the top.

  • How Do I Cancel My Membership?

    To cancel, click the “CANCEL” link at the bottom of the Account Details section at the top.

  • How Often Is New Content Added?

    5 new muscle building workouts will be added on the 1st of every month. These 5 workouts can be done every week for the entire month. 

  • I Can't See This Months Updated Workouts. What Do I Do?

    If you don’t see the new workouts then log out and sign back in. Also, you might try clearing your cache and browsing history, then refresh the page.
