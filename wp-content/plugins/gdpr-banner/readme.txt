== Description ==

This will add a banner to your website to assist with GDPR compliance, allowing you to link to your privacy policy and website terms & conditions for further information. It allows users to disable Google Analytics cookies. Please note that this is only part of the GDPR compliance process - for further information on GDPR compliance seek professional advice.
